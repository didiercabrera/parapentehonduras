Our motivation for the event is to have pilots of high level that can explore the area, exploiting the mountains, sharing knowledge while parawaiting or going XC. This for us will be very valuable in constructing a map of possible routes, gathering data that can be used for advancing the sport locally and learning from experts.
We believe that in order to grow as a community we need to be open to learn from others and leave egos aside.

If you think you are a good fit and can provide your experience to a growing community, then apply to be invited  and receive some benefits from the organization that will make your trip to Honduras more fun.

REGISTRATION WILL OPEN SOON

If you are a recipient of the benefits above, then all costs associated with them will be covered by the organization. With one exception, a fee for retrieves that go outside a designated XC area. If you end up landing outside the marked area for Cross Country, we ask for a fee per retrieval. This can be paid in advance if you think you may end up going very long. You can discuss it during the briefing with the organization.

If you don’t get selected for the benefits we are providing, you may still join the festival, but the organization won’t cover any cost. You will be responsible for your transportation, retrieves and accommodation, food and everything else.
